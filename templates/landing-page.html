About Joseph

Joseph Bahous is an experienced Investment Director at Quantacet, Canada's first quantum-focused venture capital fund based in Montréal. Since 2016, he has built a strong background in investment, covering both public and private markets, with a focus on pre-seed and seed Canadian companies in digital health and quantum technology. Joseph previously served as Investment Director at MEDTEQ+, and held analyst roles at National Bank Financial and TD. He holds a Diploma of Specialized Higher Studies and a Bachelor's in Finance from HEC Montréal, and is well-versed in CFA principles and Canadian Securities Institute certifications. Joseph is recognized for exceeding investment targets, strong portfolio performance, and active mentorship in the finance sector.
