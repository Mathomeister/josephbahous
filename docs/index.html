About Joseph

Joseph Bahous is a Montréal-based venture investor with experience across digital health, quantum technologies, and early-stage startups.

Since 2016, he has developed a strong background in both public and private markets, focusing on pre-seed and seed investments in Canadian companies shaping the future of healthcare and deep tech.

Prior to his current role, Joseph led investment efforts in digital health and held analyst positions in the financial services sector.

He holds a Bachelor's and a Diploma of Specialized Higher Studies in Finance from HEC Montréal and is a CFA charterholder.

Known for a thoughtful investment approach, collaborative portfolio support, and active mentorship, Joseph enjoys working with emerging founders and future finance professionals.

  • Expertise: Portfolio management, financial analysis, strategic investment, client relationships, market analysis, compliance, risk management
