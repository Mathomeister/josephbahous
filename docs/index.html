About Joseph

Joseph Bahous is a Montréal-based venture investor and mentor passionate about helping the next generation of founders and finance professionals. He believes in practical, honest advice and enjoys supporting students and early-career talent as they navigate the world of startups and investment.

Since 2016, Joseph has focused on pre-seed and seed investments in Canadian companies shaping the future of healthcare and deep tech. He brings experience from both public and private markets, with a strong foundation in financial analysis and portfolio management.

Joseph's mission: "To make mentorship accessible and actionable for those starting out in tech, VC, and entrepreneurship."

  • Expertise: Portfolio management, investment strategy, startup fundraising, market analysis, mentorship, and career guidance

How It Works

  1. Click "Schedule a Coffee Chat" and fill out the short application form.
  2. If you're a student or early-career professional and your request is a good fit, you'll instantly receive a Calendly link to book your chat.
  3. Book your coffee chat at a time that works for you.
  4. If your request needs manual review, Joseph will follow up by email.
  5. After your chat, you may receive follow-up resources or connections if relevant.

What to expect: A friendly, focused conversation about your goals, questions, and next steps in startups, VC, or career growth. All chats are free and confidential.
